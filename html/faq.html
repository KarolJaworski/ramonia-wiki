FAQ

Q: What's the name of main city?

Yurcity

Q: Can I buy promotion if I am not premium account?

Yes, you can. Promotion is free for everyone. You need to be level 20.

Q: Where do I buy promotion and how much it cost?

You can buy promotion in King Ramonian who is located on the top of the Temple. It costs 10k.

Q: I'm lost... What can I do to find my location?

If you have no idea where you are, simply check your location by clicking this.

Q: Where can I buy assassin stars?

You can buy assassin stars in Atrad NPC. You need to have assassin outfit to trade with him.

Q: What can I do with minor/major crystalline tokens?

You can exchange them from various items. Talk to NPC Gnomally for more info.

Q: Ramonia coins? What are those and how do I get them?

Each ramonia coin is worth 10 shop points. You can obtain them from various tasks & events.

Q: How to turn on shared experience?

To enable the shared experience, use the command "!share"

Q: What is the level difference of shared experience in party?

To enable share exp with your team partner your level difference shall not be higher than 75%.

Q: Where do I sell all the creature products?

All the creature products, including trophies, can be sold to NPC Yasir.

Q: Where can I buy flask of dust remover?

You can buy it from NPC Yaman.

Q: How does autoloot work?

By default the autoloot automatically collects all gold and potions from the monsters you've killed. There are 3 autoloot slots. You can add only stackable items to the autoloot.

Q: What are the autoloot commands?

!autoloot potions - by deafult it's on. write this command to turn it off/on
!autoloot list - shows you list of all the items, that you are currently autolooting
!autoloot clean - clears your autoloot list
!autoloot item:itemname - add an item to your autoloot (use the same command to remove your item from the list)

Q: Do I have to wear AOL when I have blesses from Avar Tar?

Yes, you have to wear AOL. Blesses don't fully protect you from item loss.

Q: How to buy blesses from Avar Tar?

To buy blesses from Avar Tar you have to either complete the Inquistion Quest or buy Avar Tar scroll from store.

Q: Can I buy blesses if I don't have the Avar Tar yet?

Yes, there are 3 blessings available:
- Norf (Yurcity temple),
- Humphrey (Ankrahmun depo),
- Eremo (Edron depo).

Q: How much do I need to pay for a training monks ticket?

Here's the formula: your_level * 50 gold coins.
Quick example: 50 lvl needs to pay 2.500 gold coins (50 * 50 = 2500).

Q: Are monks available for all professions??

Nope, monks are available for paladins & knights only. This change has been made to prevent runemaking.
