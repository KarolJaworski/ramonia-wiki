Undead Quest

Localization Requirements Monsters Reward
Yurcity 30 lvl.
Shovel, Machete, Rope
Spoiler Show/Hide

1. We are starting the quest from Yurcity temple. Head to the north-east gate.

2. Walk through the bridge and follow the map.

2. Before following the yellow line, we have to switch 3 levers (marked with the question mark).

3. Walk into the hole.

4. Walk into the hole again..

5. Walk through the gate.

6. Cut through the bush, using machete and head nort-east.

7. We encounter some ghouls and monks.

8. We encounter some ghouls, monks, dark monks and demon skeletons. Near the end, there's a warlock and a bansee.

9. Switch the lever to unlock a teleport.

10. Walk into the teleport.

11. Collect rewards.
