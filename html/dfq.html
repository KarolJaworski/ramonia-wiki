Drakens Final

Localization Requirements Monsters Reward
Port Hope 100 lvl
Drakens Task
Spoiler Show/Hide

We are starting the quest from Port Hope temple.

1. Follow the map. As soon as the blue path starts, expect to face all kind of lizards.

2. From now on, expect to face all kind of drakens.

3. You arrived at the Draken Final entrance. Good luck!

4. You don't have to use the levitate spell here. Just walk normally using your arrows.

5. Follow the path. You will encounter ghastly dragons and drakens on your way.

6. Now is the time for the real challenge! Beware adventurer, you will encounter drakens and undead dragons on your way.

7. There are several rooms like this waiting for you. Follow the map, until you reach the reward room.

You still have a chance to escape alive... Simply walk into the teleport which is marked with "X" on the map.

8. Congratulations! You have just finished the Draken Final quest!
