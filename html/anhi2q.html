Annihilator 2

Localization Requirements Monsters Reward
Yurcity 120 lvl.
4 people

Spoiler Show/Hide

1. We are starting the quest from Yurcity temple. Head to the south-west gate.

2. Walk upstairs. We encounter some leaf golems here.

3. Follow the map until you reach dragons.


4. Once you go down, you will encounter dragons and dragon hatchlings.


5. Follow the map until you reach the hole.

6. There's a dragon waiting for you in the hole. Kill him and move forward.

7. Head south-west until you reach the tower.

8. That is the Fiery Tower. We step into the hole.

9. Follow the map.

10. You will encounter dragon lords on your way.

11. Once you go down, expect to face behemoths and demons.

12. Once you step into the teleport, watch out for fire elementals and diabolic imps.

13. To pull the lever all the players have to be level 120+.

14. Clear the monsters and head east into the reward room.

15. You can choose only ONE reward! Choose wisely.
