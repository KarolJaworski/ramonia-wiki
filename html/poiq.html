We are starting the quest from Edron temple.

2. Follow the map.



3. Clear each room and collect trones. Once you clear all rooms you will have access to the door in south-west corner.

4. Example of claiming the trone.

5. Walk through the door, and step into teleport to arrive at the reward room.
