Spoiler Show/Hide

We are starting the quest from Ankrahmun depo.

1. Follow the path.


2. As soon as the blue trail starts, you will encounter: dragons, dragon lords.

3. Follow the path.

4. Watch out! When you step into the teleport, you will encounter: hellfire fighters, magma crawlers, lava golems, etc.

5. Keep following the path.

6. You arrived at the Warzone II entrance.

7. Prepare for a long and exhausting battle. Once you're ready, walk through the teleport.

8. Follow the path.

9. Prepare for a boss fight! Once you're ready, step into the teleport.

10. Defeat the deadly Gnomevil.

11. Go get your rewards!!

12. Congratulations! You've just finished Warzone II.

13. Walk into the teleport to leave the warzone.

(You will appear at the entrance of the warzone)
