Riddle Quest

Localization Requirements Monsters Reward
Yurcity 120 lvl

Spoiler Show/Hide

We are starting the quest from Yurcity temple.

1. Follow the map.

2. Watch out! From now on you will encounter hydras.

3. Watch out! As soon as the red trail starts, you will encounter behemoths.

4. Watch out! From now on you will encounter lucifers.

5. Watch out! From now on you will encounter retching horrors and choking fears.

6. That is a one way teleport - once you go in you can't go out unless you complete the quest.

7. Walk on ramonia coins - these are your safe sqms. You need to pull both levers, then just head south.

8. Walk carefully on the ramonia coins.

9. Congratulations! You finished the riddle quest.

To leave simply walk into the teleport. It will teleport you to the behemoth mountain.
