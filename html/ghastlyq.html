We are starting the quest from Yurcity temple.

1. Follow the map.

2. Sail to Desert

Hi > Desert > Yes

3. Follow the map.

4. Watch out. From now on you will encounter grim reapers and vampires.

5. Watch out. On this floor you will encounter grim reapers and ghastly dragons.

6. To unlock the stones, you have to pull 3 levers.

(Follow the map from step below)

7. The order doesn't matter. You just need to pull all of them.

(You only have about 10 minutes.)

8. First lever

9. Second lever

10. Third lever

11. You're about to enter to the final room. You will encounter 3x dragon overlord and 1x ethershreck.

TIP - Ethershreck might hit up to 10k (melee) so don't let him attack you :P

12. Claim your rewards.

13. Destroy the fire field that covers lever.

14. Pull the lever and head right.

15. Congratulations, you did it!
