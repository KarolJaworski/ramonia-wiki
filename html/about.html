About (Server Info)

Balanced Vocations

Each vocation possess new or tweaked spells. We tried our hardest to balance the vocations so that all of them would be enjoyable in PvE as well as in the PvP gameplay. Here's the list of changes:
- Druid - Poison Storm deals 80% of UE damage. Poison Storm applies physical damage.
- Knight - Fierce Berserker deals 60% higher damage than usual Berserk. Better instant healing - "Wound Cleansing".
- Paladin - New ammounition (spears, arrows, bolts). New spells - "Divine Caldera" & "Ethereal Spear".
All the tweaks were made to accelerate PvE & PvP feelings in short time.

Blessing System

There are 2 options to gain blessings:
1. Buy 3 blessings from:
- Humphrley (Ankrahmun depo) - cost 10k,
- Eremo (Edron depo) - cost 10k,
- Norf (Yurcity temple)- cost 10k.
2. Buy all blessings (5) at once from Avar Tar - cost 60k. Only available for those who finished the Inquisition Quest or bought Avar Tar scroll.
Blesses don't fully protect you from item loss, therefore both options require you to wear AOL.

Exercise Weapons

These weapons are the fastest way to reach desired skills. Eeach vocation can use it's own type of exercise weapon:
- Druid - Exercise Rod - cost 100k
- Socerer - Exercise Wand - cost 100k
- Knight - Exercise Sword | Exercise Club | Exercise Axe - cost 70k
- Paladin - Exercise Spear - cost 70k
- All Vocations - Exercise Magic - cost 70k

Exchange System

You can exchange various items like:
- Minor Crystalline Token,
- Major Crystalline Token,
- Silver Token,
- Gold Token.
For shop points, equipment, outfits, miscallaneouses. More info on each item in FAQ.

Frag System

- Time to decrease 1 frag - 10h
- Red Skull - 3 unjustified kills
- Banishment - 6 unjustified kills
- Red Skull duration - until all frags are gone
- Banishment duration - 4 days

Shared Experience System

You can trigger shared experience by using "!share" command. Shared experience gives you up to 40% bonus exp:
- 2 different vocations - 10% bonus experience,
- 3 different vocations - 20% bonus experience,
- 4 different vocations - 40% bonus experience.

Useful commands
Commands Description
[Vocation] please
example: hi > paladin please		 Use these words to leave rookmekgaard without standing in queues at the Oracle NPC.
Hi > Sell All Sell all your items from backpack (doesn't sell your equipped items).
Instant buy/sell all Sell all your items instantly without waiting in queue.
Instant buy x runes Buy any amout of runes you want instantly without waiting in queue.
x - input any amount of runes you want to buy here
!share Activate shared experience. Gives additional 10% exp bonus.
!task list Displays a list of your undergoing tasks.
!autoloot Autoloot system, for more information check the FAQ page.
!mana Shows a number of mana needed to next magic level.
!online Displays all players that are currently online.
!buyhouse Stand in front of the house in order to buy it.
!sellhouse You can sell your house. Use house ticket to trade your house to another player.
!leavehouse Immediately abandon your house. All the items from your house will be teleported to depot.
!frags Shows amount of frags you currently have.
